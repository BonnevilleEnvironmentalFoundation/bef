Last October, the Federal Trade Commission released an updated version of its Green Guides -- the agency’s environmental marketing guidelines -- for the first time since 1998. The FTC Green Guides were updated to provide clarity for consumers about what environmental claims really mean and outline parameters for the types of claims companies can make about their products. Join BEF and Cone Communications for this insightful webinar to learn more about the updated Green Guides and their implications for marketers.

 

About Jennifer Garner
